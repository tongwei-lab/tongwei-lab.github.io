About Me

I am currently a Research Fellow with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Before joining NTU, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT), University of Luxembourg. I received the Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from the University of Luxembourg in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Bhavani Shankar M. R.

My research focuses on integrated sensing and communication (ISAC), radar signal processing, near-field sensing, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, holographic MIMO, beamforming design, target localization, channel estimation, and convex/nonconvex optimization for 6G wireless systems.

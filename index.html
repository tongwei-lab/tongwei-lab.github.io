I am currently a Research Fellow with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Before joining NTU, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher with the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT), University of Luxembourg, from Nov. 2024 to Mar. 2026.

I received the Ph.D. degree in Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT), University of Luxembourg in Nov. 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Bhavani Shankar M. R. I was also a visiting Ph.D. student at National Tsing Hua University from Aug. 2024 to Nov. 2024, supervised by Prof. Chong-Yung Chi. I received the M.Eng. degree from Shenzhen University in 2020 and the B.Eng. degree from Hainan University in 2017.
[Find out more]

My general research interests include integrated sensing and communication (ISAC), near-field sensing and localization, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, holographic MIMO, radar signal processing, convex and nonconvex optimization, and machine learning for 6G wireless systems.
